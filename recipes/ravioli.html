Ravioli

Description

Raviolis are square or round pillow shaped stuffed pastas, similar to dumplings. They have a filling that can consist of cheese, meat, and/or vegetables

Ravioli are formed by having one layer of flat pasta topped with filling and then placing another flat layer on top to form a pillow.

Ingredients

    For the dough: All-purpose flour, salt, eggs, water, and olive oil

    For the filling: Ricotta, Parmesan, mozzarella and provolone cheeses, an egg, and dried parsley

    For the sauce: Olive oil, garlic, prepared basil pesto sauce (you can use store-bought or homemade sauce), heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and marinara sauce
